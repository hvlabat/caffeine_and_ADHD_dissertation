asrs.csv

- contains participant level data on responses to the ASRS questionnaire. The questionnaire has two subscales. Column headers are self-explanatory. Reference for ASRS in Hampsey et al (2019)

microsaccades_trials.csv

- contains trial level data on microsacccade counts. Columns are

filename: contains participant number and trial number
N: microsaccade count (an integer, can be 0)
trial_length: in seconds


details in Hampsey et al (2019)
